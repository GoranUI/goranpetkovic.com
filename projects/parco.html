ParCo — Parking Marketplace Mobile App

2025

  • Product Design
  • UX Research
  • UI
  • Mobile
  • Figma

Overview

ParCo is an Airbnb-style parking marketplace for Belgrade, connecting drivers with private garage owners to solve the city's critical shortage of 664,092 vehicles competing for just 34,000 parking spaces.

Project brief

Design a user-centered mobile platform enabling drivers to discover, book, and navigate to available parking in real time while giving garage owners tools to list and manage their spaces.

Role

As a lead product designer, I conducted user research with 60–80 participants, developed personas, defined MVP features, and iterated through multiple rounds of wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing.

Methods

  • Design thinking process with user interviews, surveys, and observational studies.
  • Multiple prototype iterations based on real user feedback.
  • Progressive disclosure via a modal system to solve availability clarity between "park now" vs "reserve later" use cases.
