Overview

Twilight is a ZK-rollup-based, privacy-first Bitcoin perpetuals exchange — the only Perp DEX where trading accounts are anonymous by default, making P&L untraceable even after execution.

Project brief

Design the complete product UX from the ground up, including the CCA auction dashboard, trading interface, price discovery, order management, and marketing website, making highly complex crypto mechanics accessible to real users.

Role

As a sole product designer, I oversaw end-to-end UX/UI, including information architecture, user flows, dashboard design, component system, and Framer website development.

Methods

  • Mapped complex auction mechanics (concurrent combinatorial auctions) into scannable dashboard layouts.
  • Used progressive disclosure to surface critical data (clearing price, FDV, and bid progress) without overwhelming users.
  • Dark visual language reinforced privacy positioning throughout.
